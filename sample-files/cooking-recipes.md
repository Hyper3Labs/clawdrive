# Quick Weeknight Recipes

## Garlic Butter Pasta
Cook spaghetti. Saute minced garlic in butter. Toss with pasta, parmesan, red pepper flakes. 15 minutes.

## Sheet Pan Chicken
Season chicken thighs with paprika. Surround with broccoli and sweet potato. Roast at 425F for 25 minutes.

## Thai Basil Stir Fry
Hot wok, oil, garlic, chili. Add ground pork, fish sauce, sugar. Finish with Thai basil leaves. Serve over rice.
