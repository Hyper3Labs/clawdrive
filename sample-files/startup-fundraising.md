# Startup Fundraising Guide

## Pre-Seed Round
Raise from angels and micro-VCs. Typical: $250K-$1M at $3-8M valuation. Focus on team and vision.

## Seed Round
Product-market fit signals needed. Typical: $1-4M at $8-20M valuation. Show traction metrics.

## Series A
Proven business model. Typical: $5-15M at $30-80M valuation. Need repeatable growth engine.
